ready for a bright future?

Complete your free online application and begin your journey to a college where you truly belong.

Simply create an account or log in to continue a previous application.

application faqs

Applications for all Alverno programs are currently accepted and reviewed on a rolling basis, so you can apply at any time.

Applying to Alverno is 100% free.

To complete your application, you will need your official transcripts from all institutions attended (transcripts and e-transcripts are "official" when they come in a sealed envelope and direct email from the issuing institution)

Once you have submitted your completed application and we have received your required documents, you can expect to hear back within around two weeks.

An admissions counselor will get in touch and let you know that your application has been reviewed.
